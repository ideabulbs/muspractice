muspractice - musician's practicing assistant

muspractice is an experimental tool allowing you to organize your music practice sessions and improve retention rates of the musical material you are studying.

muspractice lets you practice in scheduled play-along sessions of limited length. You create your sessions yourself, deciding on your priorities.

Features

  • Manage a library of audioclips, repeatable chunks of music material, intended to play along with.
  • Each chunk (called 'phrase') can be practiced using the spaced repetition technique
  • Scheduling and prioritization allow you maintain a long backlog of learning items
  • Set custom phrase speed and key
  • Save metronome presets (duration, speed, meter)
  • Phrases and metronome presets can have automatic speed-increments to match your improving skill at a given piece of musical material
